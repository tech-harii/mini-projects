HOW TO MAKE A BURGER?

Homepage

Description

Burger is a popular fast-food dish made with a soft bun filled with a cooked patty, fresh vegetables, and sauces.

It is simple to prepare and can be customized with different fillings and flavors.


Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground meat or veggie patty
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Season and cook the patty in a pan until fully done.
  2. Lightly toast the burger buns.
  3. Place the patty on the bottom bun.
  4. Add cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  5. Spread sauce on top.
  6. Cover with the top bun.
  7. Serve hot.
