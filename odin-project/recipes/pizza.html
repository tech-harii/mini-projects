HOW TO MAKE A PIZZA?

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients like vegetables, meats, or herbs

. It is baked until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.


Ingridents

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil to form a soft dough.
  2. Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours.
  3. Roll the dough into a round base.
  4. Spread tomato sauce on top.
