HOW TO MAKE FRIED RICE?

Homepage

Description

Fried Rice is a popular Asian dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, sauces, and sometimes meat or eggs.

It is cooked in a pan or wok until everything is well mixed and flavorful.


Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, capsicum)
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Egg or chicken (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until slightly tender.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  5. Pour soy sauce, add salt and pepper.
  6. Stir-fry everything for 3–5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
